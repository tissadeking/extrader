How the System Works

EXTrader, short for Executive Trader is a forex trading system that has been developed and embedded with the ability to administer and manage trades automatically. That implies that it executes your trades without your input. You can use it even if you have no prior knowledge about forex.

EXTrader has been tested and confirmed to have over 80% accuracy in forex trading.

Four Steps for EXTrader Usage

  1. Create a Metatrader 4 or 5 (MT4 or MT5) account for trading forex pairs
  2. Register with EXTrader
  3. Download the EXTrader app that would run on the MT4 or MT5 app
  4. Activate it to trade on your behalf
